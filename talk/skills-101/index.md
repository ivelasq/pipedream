## Description

Throughout this conference, you're going to hear the word "skill" a lot. These specifically mean "agent skills", specialized tools and behaviors passed to large language models to make them actionable. If you've found yourself wondering, "What actually is a skill?"" then join us for Skills 101. We'll break down what they are, what they are not, and how they work, giving you the foundational toolkit to navigate the rest of the conference.
