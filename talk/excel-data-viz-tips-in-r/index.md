## Description

Have you ever seen a nifty Excel data visualization and wondered, "Can I do that with R?" The answer is likely yes! With R, you can create stunning, customizable visuals that elevate your data analysis. In this talk, we will cover seven data visualization tips that are achievable in Excel, but also demonstrate how (and why) to create them in R. From sparklines to heat maps, if Excel can do it, R can do it too! Best of all, your work will be reproducible and repeatable, allowing you to share your code and remake your charts anytime, making your work more transparent and streamlined. Join us to learn the tools for making impactful and reproducible dataviz in R!
